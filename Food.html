Budgie Diet & Exercise

Feeding Your Budgie

Budgies thrive on a balanced and varied diet. While seed mixes are common, they should be supplemented with other options to ensure your bird stays healthy and happy.

  • Pellets: A high-quality pellet mix should make up the bulk of their diet.
  • Fresh Vegetables: Leafy greens like spinach, kale, and carrots are excellent choices.
  • Fruits: Offer small amounts of apple, banana, or berries (avoid seeds and pits).
  • Fresh Water: Provide clean water daily and clean the dish regularly.
  • Avoid: Avocados, chocolate, caffeine, alcohol, and salty or sugary foods.

Exercise & Enrichment

Budgies are active birds that need daily exercise to stay physically and mentally fit. A bored budgie can develop behavioral issues, so keeping them engaged is essential.

  • Flying Time: Allow your budgie to fly in a safe, enclosed space for at least 30 minutes daily.
  • Toys: Provide a variety of toys—bells, mirrors, ladders, and swings—to stimulate their minds.
  • Interaction: Spend time talking and playing with your budgie to strengthen your bond.
  • Perches: Use natural wood perches of varying thickness to promote foot health.
  • Rotate Toys: Change toys weekly to keep things interesting and prevent boredom.

Healthy budgies are happy budgies!
With the right diet and plenty of exercise, your budgie will thrive and bring joy to your home.
