Where to Find Budgies

  • Local Animal Shelters: Some shelters have birds available for adoption, call your local shelters and see if there are any bird up for adoption.
  • Bird Rescues: Specialized organizations that rehome budgies and other parrots, while harder to care for depending on the birds background and where it came from, it is rewarding to see it slowly warm up to you.
  • Pet Stores: Budgies are available in most pet stores like petco, but check for ethical sourcing and healthy conditions.
  • Breeders: Offer specific breeds and hand-raised budgies with known backgrounds, look for breeders in your area for more information.
